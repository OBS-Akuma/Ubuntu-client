FAQ
Is Ubuntu client safe?
Yes, Ubuntu client is safe as can be as it's open source
Does this client use my token?
Yes, we use your token for API calling only for the official Kirka API.
Is this client named after the operating system?
Yes, I couldn't think of a new Client name and used the operating Ubuntu as the client name.
Can i have a custom Badge or gradient?
Most likely Not, The idea is to give out badges to users if they are well known or they did something to help me or the game.
Will I get banned for using Ubuntu client?
Not that we're aware of. If Xip or kirka staff flags something, we will take action.
How do i get small guns?
Go to the Plugins tab of the in-game menu And you will find it, it's named "Weapon Customization"
How do i Chnage my Gun texture?
Open your pc files and go to documents/Ubuntu/assets/img Then add your skin image with the name of the Texture you want to switch it to.
